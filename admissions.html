Welcome to SMACOMA Admissions

St. Margaret College Makerere (SMACOMA) is committed to nurturing future leaders through quality education rooted in values and innovation. Our admissions process is designed to identify passionate, capable students ready to join our vibrant community.

Whether you're aiming for O-Level, A-Level, or our specialized certificate programs, we offer a supportive environment to help you thrive. Explore this page for detailed information on how to apply, what we require, and the costs involved.

Application Process

Joining SMACOMA is simple and transparent. Follow these steps to start your journey:

  1. Download or Collect the Form: Access it below or visit our admissions office at Makerere Hill Road, Kampala.
  2. Complete Your Application: Fill out all sections, ensuring accuracy in personal and academic details.
  3. Gather Documents: Prepare the required documents listed below.
  4. Pay the Application Fee: UGX 50,000 (non-refundable) via bank deposit or mobile money (details provided on the form).
  5. Submit Your Application: Email to admissions@smacoma.ac.ug or deliver in person by the deadline.
  6. Interview & Assessment: Shortlisted candidates attend an interview and entrance exam.
  7. Admission Offer: Successful applicants receive an offer letter within two weeks.

Note: Applications are processed on a rolling basis, so early submission is encouraged!

Admission Requirements

We seek students with academic potential, character, and a drive to excel. Here are the cool requirements:

  • O-Level Entry: Minimum of 5 passes (C4 or better) in UNEB exams, including English and Mathematics. Bonus points for distinction in Sciences or Arts!
  • A-Level Entry: At least 2 principal passes at A-Level, with a strong O-Level background (5+ passes).
  • Certificate Programs: PLE certificate with a pass in English, plus a creative portfolio or practical skills demo (e.g., art, tech, or leadership).
  • Documents:
    • Certified copies of academic transcripts/certificates.
    • Two passport-sized photos (recent, with your name on the back).
    • Birth certificate or national ID.
    • Recommendation letter from your last school’s headteacher.
    • Proof of application fee payment.
  • Extra Edge: Participation in extracurriculars (sports, debate, music) or community service boosts your application!

Application Deadlines

Mark your calendar to secure your spot:

  • First Intake (February 2025): December 15, 2024
  • Second Intake (June 2025): May 15, 2025
  • Late Applications: Accepted until January 15, 2025 (First Intake) or June 1, 2025 (Second Intake) with a UGX 20,000 late fee.

Early applicants get priority in scholarship considerations and dormitory allocation!

Scholarships & Financial Aid

We believe in rewarding talent and supporting need. Explore our scholarship options:

  • Merit Scholarship: Up to 50% tuition waiver for students with 8+ distinctions at O-Level or 3 principal passes (A-B) at A-Level.
  • Need-Based Aid: Partial fee coverage for financially challenged students (submit proof of income).
  • Sports & Arts Excellence: 20–30% discount for national-level achievers in sports, music, or art.
  • How to Apply: Include a scholarship request letter with your application.

Contact our bursar at bursar@smacoma.ac.ug for more details.
