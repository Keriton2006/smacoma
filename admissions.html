Application Process

At St. Margaret College Makerere, we make the application process straightforward and accessible. Follow these steps to apply:

  1. Download the Application Form: Get the form below or visit our office.
  2. Complete the Form: Fill in all required details accurately.
  3. Submit Documents: Attach the necessary documents (see requirements below).
  4. Pay Application Fee: A non-refundable fee of UGX 50,000 is required.
  5. Submit Application: Send your application via email to admissions@smacoma.edu or drop it at our admissions office.

Admission Requirements

We welcome applications from students who meet the following criteria:

  • Completed O-Level or A-Level with a minimum of 5 passes, including English and Mathematics.
  • Certified copies of academic transcripts and certificates.
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs.
  • A copy of the applicant's birth certificate or national ID.
  • Recommendation letter from the previous school (optional).

Application Deadlines

Ensure you submit your application before the following deadlines:

  • First Intake (January): November 30, 2024
  • Second Intake (June): April 30, 2025

Late applications may be considered on a case-by-case basis.
