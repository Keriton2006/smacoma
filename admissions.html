Admissions Overview

St. Margaret College Makerere (SMACOMA), located in Makerere Kikoni off Sir Apollo Kaggwa Road, is a mixed, day, and boarding secondary school registered with the Ministry of Education & Sports (Center No. U1883). We offer Arts and Sciences at O and A Levels, alongside vocational training in Tailoring, Catering, Hairdressing, Fruit Processing, and Computer Training (DIT Center No. UVQF/811).

Our vibrant community nurtures talents through co-curricular activities like football, netball, volleyball, basketball, chess, and athletics. Join us to embark on a journey of academic excellence and personal growth.

Admission Requirements

We seek passionate and capable students. Requirements include:

  • O-Level Entry: 5 passes (C4 or better) in UNEB exams, including English and Mathematics.
  • A-Level Entry: 2 principal passes at A-Level, 5+ O-Level passes.
  • Documents:
    • Certified academic transcripts/certificates.
    • Primary Leaving Recommendation (O-Level) or UCE pass slip (A-Level).
    • Passport photos (UGX 5,000, one for student, one for parent).
    • Birth certificate or national ID.
    • Recommendation letter from previous school.
    • Stamped medical form from a qualified medical officer.
    • Signed rules and regulations form.
  • Additional Items:
    • Uniform: O-Level (UGX 65,000), A-Level (UGX 70,000).
    • School T-shirt (UGX 30,000), sportswear (UGX 30,000), sweater (UGX 40,000).
    • Casual wear for boarders (UGX 50,000).
    • Black shoes (low-heeled), black belt, white socks (girls).
    • Textbooks, black books, stationery (Rotatrim for S.1–S.4, ruled ream for S.5–S.6).
    • 2 brooms, 6 toilet papers.

Scholarships

We offer:

  • Merit Scholarship: Up to 50% tuition waiver for 8+ distinctions (O-Level) or 3 principal passes (A-B, A-Level).
  • Need-Based Aid: Partial fee coverage (submit proof of income).
  • Sports & Arts: 20–30% discount for national-level achievers.
  • Application: Include a scholarship request letter.
