School Overview

St. Margaret College Makerere (SMACOMA) is a premier mixed, day, and boarding secondary school registered with the Ministry of Education & Sports (Center No. U1883). Located in Makerere Kikoni, we provide a holistic education that combines academic excellence with personal development.

Our mission is to nurture future leaders through quality education, discipline, and co-curricular engagement. SMACOMA offers Arts and Sciences at O and A Levels, alongside vocational training programs certified by the Directorate of Industrial Training (DIT Center No. UVQF/811).

Founded in 1995, SMACOMA has grown into a vibrant community, fostering talents in sports, arts, and leadership. Our alumni excel in various fields, contributing to national and global development.

Facilities

SMACOMA boasts state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and extracurricular activities:

  • Science Laboratories: Fully equipped for Biology, Chemistry, and Physics experiments.
  • Computer Labs: Modern computers with internet access for ICT training.
  • Library: Extensive collection of academic and recreational books.
  • Sports Facilities: Fields for football, netball, volleyball, and athletics tracks.
  • Boarding Houses: Comfortable dormitories with 24/7 supervision.
  • Vocational Workshops: Spaces for tailoring, catering, and hairdressing training.
  • Chapel: For spiritual growth and reflection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common inquiries about SMACOMA:

  • What is the application process? Download the form, complete it, gather documents, pay the fee, submit, attend an interview, and receive an offer.
  • Are scholarships available? Yes, we offer merit, need-based, and sports/arts scholarships. Contact our scholarship coordinator for details.
  • What are the boarding requirements? Boarders need uniforms, bedding, personal items, and stationery as listed in the admissions section.
  • Can parents visit? Yes, parents can visit during designated visiting days or by appointment.
  • Is transport provided? SMACOMA offers optional school bus services for day scholars within Kampala.
