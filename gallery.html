About SMACOMA

Established in 1954, St. Margaret College Makerere (SMACOMA) is a cornerstone of educational excellence in Uganda. With a mission to nurture principled leaders, we blend academic rigor with holistic development, serving over 1,280 students and supported by 32 dedicated staff. Our alumni, exceeding 740 graduates, include influential leaders in government, business, and academia.

Our campus in Makerere features cutting-edge facilities: advanced science labs, a digital library, a performing arts center, and expansive sports fields. In 2025, we introduced a robotics program and expanded our vocational training, empowering students for diverse career paths. SMACOMA’s community outreach includes free literacy workshops, impacting over 500 local families annually.

  • Comprehensive Academics

    Sciences, humanities, and vocational courses tailored for global competitiveness.

  • Dynamic Extracurriculars

    Clubs for robotics, drama, and music; sports like soccer and basketball.

  • Notable Achievements

    2025 National Debate Champions, top science fair awards, and 98% pass rate.

  • Community Impact

    Annual charity drives and environmental projects fostering social responsibility.
